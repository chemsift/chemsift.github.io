About ChemSift

ChemSift leverages AI and data-driven models to enable faster, smarter, and more equitable drug discovery. Our goal is to make chemical intelligence accessible and useful for research teams worldwide.

Our Products

From cheminformatics tools to predictive analytics pipelines, ChemSift provides open and adaptable technology for computational chemistry and bioinformatics.

Compound Insights

Analyze molecular datasets with AI-driven pattern recognition.

Reaction Predictor

Predict reaction outcomes and design better synthesis routes.

Data Harmonizer

Unify experimental and computational data across sources.

Our Team

We are a group of scientists, engineers, and open science advocates working to accelerate innovation in chemistry.
